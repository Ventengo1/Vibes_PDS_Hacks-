Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join PDS Hacks?

Absolutely anyone! Whether you've never written a line of code or you build apps in your free time, our workshops are designed to accommodate all skill levels.

When and where do we meet?

We meet every Tuesday during US community time in the Upper School tech lab.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring your school laptop and your creativity! We provide everything else, including software tools, hardware parts, and snacks.

Ready to start building?

No prior coding experience required. We meet on days 5/7 during US community time in the CS Lab or nearby rooms (just schoology for info).
